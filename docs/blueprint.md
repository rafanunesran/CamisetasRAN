# **App Name**: UniformeGestor

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display school uniform products with details such as sizes, materials, and prices.
- Order Placement: Enable customers (schools/resellers) to place orders through the platform.
- Inventory Management: Manage stock levels, track incoming shipments, and monitor stock turnover.
- Customer Management: Maintain a database of schools and resellers, including contact details and order history.
- Production Tracking: Monitor the status of each uniform's production, from material allocation to completion.
- Commission Calculation: Calculate commissions for schools and payment amounts for sales representatives and production staff.
- Reporting and Analytics: Provide insights into sales trends, popular products, and commission payouts using charts and reports.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#2E3A87) to evoke trust and professionalism, ideal for a school-related platform.
- Background color: Light gray (#E9ECEF), providing a neutral backdrop that enhances the visibility of the products.
- Accent color: Muted purple (#9B5DE5) to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, will provide a balance of modern and warm feel that suits the target audience.
- Use clear and simple icons to represent different functionalities and product categories.
- Employ a grid-based layout for a clean and organized presentation of products and data.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to improve user engagement and provide feedback.
